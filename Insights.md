# OBSERVATIONS

1. Average customer age is 41.39.
2. Customers within the age group of 40-43 spent the most.
3. Female customers have higher spending average than male customers.
4. Electronics items generated the highest revenue.
5. Beauty products are sold the most but they generate lowest revenue.
6. Month of May generated the highest revenue seeing 23rd May, 2023 as the maximum revenue collection day.
7. Clothing products are purhcased in more quantity but generate a lowe revenue as compared to Electronics.
8. The minimum transaction was about $25 and the maximum was $2000.

# INSIGHTS

1. We should target the 40-43 age group using promotions, loyalty programs and advertisements for quality products.
2. We should have more products according to the female customer preferences as they have a higher spending average than the male customers.
3. We use clothes as the medium to attract customers as they are a necessity and then cross sell electronics with them.
4. Month of May having a high revenue could depend on the season at that time or if there was any particular festival.
5. Since minimum transaction amount was $25 and maximum was $2000, we should recommend them options so that they can go from $25 to $50 and for those with maximum spending, we should create some VIP programs for them.
6. Beauty products show high volume but low revenue. If this category has repeat purchase behavior or customers return frequently for beauty items, this could actually work as a retention and loyalty driver despite lower per-transaction revenue.
